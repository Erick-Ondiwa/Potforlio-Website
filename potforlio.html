AgroCare

Project Description:
AgroCare is an Agricultural Website designed To bridge the gap between input supply as well as access to skilled agricultural personnel. It allows farmers to easily create their personal accounts to be eligible to hire farm inputs and/or skilled agricultural personnel.
Role and Responsibilities:
Full-Stack Developer responsible for designing the front-end interface, specifically the products page, which displays a list of products they could add to basket developing the back-end API, and integrating the database.
